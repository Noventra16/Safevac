🎯 What SafeVac does
SafeVac is India's universal building evacuation app — for every school, hospital, office, mall, apartment and public building across the country.

Enter your building below. The app auto-locates it via GPS, generates floor-wise evacuation plans, gives 7 emergency protocols with Do/Don't guides, and lets you trigger SOS with one tap — even without internet.
